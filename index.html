ABOUT US

ALOB Engineering Concepts Limited is a fully indigenous Mechanical, Electrical, Steel and Allied Fabrication Company. Established in 2016, but incorporated by Corporate Affairs commission in March 2018.

Over this time, we have worked in various cities across the country and currently in different stages of completion of works for our various clients (as would be highlighted in ‘Our Projects’ list).

We have maintained professionalism and have worked with integrity and we can be trusted for that minimum in our dealings with all our clients.

Team work within and outside the organization is of utmost importance to us and this is displayed by our keenness to creating masterpieces to our clients’ expectations.

Chief Operating Officer

Profile – Agbato, Luqmon (Engr.)

Luqmon, holds a Bachelor of Engineering Degree in Mechanical Engineering, from University of Ilorin, Kwara State. He is a member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE), a registered member of Council of Registered Engineers of Nigeria, member of the Project Management Institute (PMI), and a certified Project Management Professional (PMP).


He has worked across discplines (Mechanical ,Electrical and Civil) and acquired superior knowledge MEP Installations and steel works such as fabrication and installation of ducted HVAC systems, chilled water/air exchange tanks for factories, Fire Fighting systems, Diesel tank systems and all sanitary fittings,all Electrical works (LV&ELV) in internal and external installations for buildings and industries.

He has attended several trainings, conferences, and courses including Project Management Conferences and Key Executive Program at Harvard Business School, USA.

Technical Partner

Profile – Emeka Eruchalu (Engr.)

Emeka holds a Bachelor of Engineering Degree in Civil Engineering from University of Nigeria Nsukka and obtained Master of Engineering Degree from University Of Ilorin. He is a member of Nigeria Society of Engineers, Project Management Institute and a registered Engineer b Council for Regulation of Engineering Practices in Nigeria (COREN).


He is Design Engineer with over twenty five years’ experience in design of institutional, industrial & residential buildings, office complexes, highway structures as well as multi-purpose facilities as petrol retail outlets. Including an extensive computational experience in numerical analysis and computer graphics.

This has culminated in the development of an application package for the analysis and design of basic structural systems including- building frames, continuous beams, columns, footings, retaining / sheet-pile walls and piled foundations.
