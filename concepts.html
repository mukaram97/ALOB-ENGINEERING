The Alob Engineering Concepts Processes

Engineering Concepts Limited is dedicated to the use of international standard equipment to undertake all our tasks and project work in the achievement of high precision Mechanical, Electrical, Steel and allied installations and Industrial Engineering Services. We are also dedicated to creating high productive teams focused on top quality performances during material procurement, Installation, transportation, Steel fabrication, and erection. This is the key to our plan for repeat jobs from our Clients.

QA / QC

We are currently working to have full ISO 9001:2008 certification and other relevant certifications. Our continuous strive for excellence would keep us in tune with our Suppliers and Specialist sub-contractors, and ensure that all our products and services meet all requisite certification standards.

Safety

We are convinced that Health, Safety, and Environment should be preserved, thus our tolerance for safety violations is a ZERO. We support the global concern on Health, Safety, and Environment and immediate adaptation to the laid down rules of Main Contractors’ and Client’s HSE objectives on site. In preparing for any project, Alob Engineering Concepts reviews, in detail, the scope and the level of the project, to ensure that identifiable hazards and control measures are put in place, so as to ensure that our HSE objectives and that of the Client/Main Contractor is accomplished. In line with this, our staff and management are periodically trained and re-trained to conform to the basic HSE standard requirement.
